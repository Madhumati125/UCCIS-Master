Principal Secretary Operational Intelligence Dashboard

Project Overview
The UCCIS Governance Command Center is an executive-level operational intelligence dashboard designed for Principal Secretary level monitoring. The platform focuses on governance visibility, escalation management, operational continuity, telemetry monitoring, replay visibility, and executive cognition speed.
Core Objectives

- Reduce scrolling and dashboard fragmentation
- Improve executive scanability within 3–5 seconds
- Create governance-focused operational visualization
- Improve information density and hierarchy
- Preserve observability and replay continuity

  Design Reasoning
  The dashboard uses dense but structured layouts inspired by mission-control interfaces and observability systems. Large KPI cards are placed first for instant cognition. Timeline systems preserve operational continuity. Heatmaps visualize escalation density rapidly. Color hierarchy improves executive decision speed.
